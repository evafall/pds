About Us

Preksha design studio is an architecture, urban design and interior design firm based in Bengaluru. We are a young design firm with a combined experience of 60+ years.

Our Vision

To be a professional & customer-centric company.

Who we are?

We aspire to provide design solutions rich in innovation, simplicity and practical approach, for built environment that contribute to the sense of well-being and increase the productivity of the end users and make a vast improvement in their lives.

Our Aim

To be recognized as one of the best architects.

What we do?

Our team possesses high critical thinking skills; intelligent design and thorough involvement in the design build process.
Our core team has the experience & expertise in handling large scale residential, commercial, institutional, corporate, hospitality and interior projects all over India in the last 30 years. The team has delivered over 4.0 Million Sq Ft of design projects pan India
